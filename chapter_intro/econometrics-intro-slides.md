## Slide 4: Enter the Data Detective

### Your Econometric Toolkit:
1. **Correlation ≠ Causation**
   - Ice cream sales and drownings both rise in summer
   - Cities with more police have more crime (wait, what?)

2. **Quantifying Uncertainty**
   - Not "will this policy work?" but "what are the odds?"
   - Confidence intervals: your hedge against overconfidence

3. **Natural Experiments**
   - Vietnam draft lottery → effects of military service [Angrist, 1990]
   - Arbitrary school district boundaries → returns to education

## Slide 5: Modern Victories Against Nonsense

### Econometrics That Changed Minds:
- **Card & Krueger (1994):** Minimum wage increase didn't kill jobs in NJ
- **Raj Chetty:** Moving to better neighborhoods transforms kids' life outcomes
- **Esther Duflo:** RCTs showing what actually works in development [Nobel Prize, 2019]

**These studies didn't just argue better—they brought better data**
